Exploring the Career of Tate McRae

Tate McRae is a Canadian pop singer-songwriter and dancer born on July 1, 2003 in Calgary, Canada. Being an incredibly skilled dancer, Tate is known for her captivating and energetic stage presence.

Early Life and Career

Tate McRae first gained popularity in the dance world after her appearance on So You Think You Can Dance: The Next Generation in 2016 as the first Canadian finalist. In 2017, Tate started a Youtube series called “Create with Tate” where her video of her song “One Day” went viral with over 40 million views, launching her music career.

Top of the Charts

Tate McRae’s debut studio album was number three on Canadian music charts containing the hit song “she’s all i wanna be” that made it to the top 10 on several international charts. In 2023, her hit single “greedy” made it to Billboard Global 200 and was number three on Hot 100. Her album “Think Later” followed shortly after which debuted in the top five in multiple countries. Her third, most recent studio album, “So Close to What” released in 2025 debuted at number 1 on Billboard 200, and became the biggest streaming week of her career. This album prompted Tate’s Miss Possessive Tour, which is her very first arena tour. Her song “Just Keep Watching” was also played in the new 2025 movie, F1, which made it to the top of many charts.

    Tate's Signature Style

    Growing up in a hockey-loving household, Tate incorporated the sporty look into her everyday style and on stage. She wears custom jerseys with her name on the back along with some chunky, knee-high boots. She even sells her own merchandise embracing the sporty style so that her fans can connect with her and her brand. This style has become a trend on social media, especially as younger audiences tend to prefer a more relaxed but fashionable look.
